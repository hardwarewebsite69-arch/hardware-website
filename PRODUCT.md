# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Kenyan contractors, construction engineers, hardware retailers, and individual homeowners/builders. They are often browsing on mobile devices (frequently under-powered or with limited data) and are looking for specific, reliable products with transparent (or easily accessible) pricing.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a digital storefront and trust-building platform for Amroz Traders. The site should showcase a vast catalog of hardware, electrical, plumbing, and building materials, encouraging users to request bulk quotes or contact the business directly via WhatsApp.

## Brand Personality
Confident, industrial, and practical. It should feel like a premium, established Kenyan business that understands the local market and prioritizes reliability and professional service.

## Anti-references
- Minimalist "Silicon Valley" SaaS landing pages (too abstract).
- Cluttered, low-quality e-commerce sites with generic stock photos.
- Sites that hide contact info or make WhatsApp communication difficult.
- Overly decorative "ghost-cards" and tiny gray text.

## Design Principles
- **Built for Trust**: Every section should reinforce reliability (ISO certs, delivery, quality).
- **Industrial Clarity**: Use bold typography and clear hierarchy to make product specs easy to read.
- **Mobile-First Utility**: Ensure high tap targets and fast load times for Kenyan mobile users.
- **Path to WhatsApp**: All roads lead to a helpful WhatsApp interaction after trust is established.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- High contrast for readability in various lighting conditions (common for site visits).
- Large touch targets for one-handed mobile use.
- Optimized for lower bandwidth (efficient image delivery).
